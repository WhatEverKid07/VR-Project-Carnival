VR Carnival Fiesta

A short 3-week group project making a carnival-themed VR game.

About

We managed to make two carnival games, a duck shooter and hook-a-duck (we liked ducks), along with a carnival pier environment.

Process

Art/Visuals: We had a dedicated 2D and 3D artist to create the assets. Both artists were very pleased with the results, and so was I.

Sound/Music: Due to our short time frame, we never properly discussed anything audio-related, so we don't have any music or sound effects.

Code: Creating a VR game was relatively straightforward in most aspects, as many of the essential scripts were provided in a demo scene. I used the core scripts and objects required to make an object grabbable and adapted them to suit our game. Since most of our mechanics were simple, a short tutorial was enough for me to get started.
